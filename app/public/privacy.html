Privacy Policy

Pindejos Bowling League — Mobile Application

Overview

The Pindejos Bowling League operates a private mobile application ("Pindejos," the "app") used by league members to track weekly matchups, scores, standings, RSVPs, season statistics, and the in-app social game economy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Pindejos is a private app for a single recreational bowling league. It is not directed at the general public and is not intended for use by children under 13.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the league app. We do not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

  • Mobile phone number. Used as your identity for sign-in. The app uses one-time passcode (OTP) authentication delivered via SMS — there is no password.
  • Account & profile information. Your player record in the league roster (such as your name as it appears in the league) and your role (player or administrator).
  • League and gameplay content. Information you create or that is recorded as part of league play — matchups, scores, standings, RSVPs, statistics, and in-app economy activity (such as pins, bets, loans, bounties, and auctions).
  • Identifiers. An internal user identifier that links your account to your league and gameplay content.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or device advertising identifiers, and we do not sell your personal information.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected by the app is used solely to provide league functionality:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure (OTP login).
  • To record and display matchups, scores, standings, RSVPs, and statistics.
  • To operate the in-app social game economy (pins, bets, loans, bounties, auctions).
  • To administer the league (for example, distinguishing players from administrators).

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing, and we do not perform cross-app or cross-site tracking.

How Your Information Is Stored

League data is stored in a secure cloud Postgres database hosted by Supabase, our backend and data-storage provider. Access to the database is restricted, and data is protected in transit using encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections.

Service Providers

We share limited information with service providers only as needed to operate the app. We do not sell or rent your information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

  • Supabase — hosts the application database and authentication. All league and account data is stored here.
  • Twilio — delivers the SMS one-time passcode used for login. Your phone number is provided to Twilio only to send the verification text.

These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for their own purposes.

SMS Messages

The app sends transactional SMS only — one-time passcode login codes triggered by your own sign-in attempt. No marketing or promotional texts are sent. Message and data rates may apply; message frequency varies. For details on SMS consent and opt-out, see our SMS Consent Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your account and league data for as long as you are a member of the league and the app remains in operation, or until you request removal. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information at any time by contacting the league administrator below.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Opt out of SMS / login: Reply STOP to any login text, or contact the administrator to have your number removed. Note that this disables login access to the app.
  • Access or deletion: Contact the administrator to request a copy of, correction to, or deletion of your personal information.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for adult league members. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date below.
