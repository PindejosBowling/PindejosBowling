SMS Consent Policy

Pindejos Bowling League — Mobile Application

Program Description

The Pindejos Bowling League operates a private mobile application ("Pindejos") used by league members to track weekly matchups, scores, standings, RSVPs, and season statistics.

As part of the sign-in flow, the application uses one-time passcode (OTP) authentication delivered via SMS text message. This allows members to securely verify their identity using their registered mobile phone number without a password.

How Consent Is Obtained

SMS consent is obtained at the point of league registration. When an individual joins the Pindejos Bowling League, they provide their mobile phone number and are informed that:

  • Their phone number will be used to send OTP login codes for the Pindejos app.
  • Consent to receive these messages is a condition of accessing the app.
  • Consent may be revoked at any time by contacting the league administrator.

Opt-in method: Verbal or written consent is collected by the league administrator when a player's phone number is registered in the system. Players are not added without their knowledge. No phone numbers are sourced from third parties.

Nature of Messages Sent

The Pindejos application sends transactional SMS messages only. Specifically:

  • OTP login codes — a short numeric code sent when a member attempts to sign in.

No marketing, promotional, or unsolicited messages are sent. Messages are triggered exclusively by the member's own login action.

Message Frequency

Members receive one SMS message per login attempt. Message frequency varies based on individual usage. Most members receive fewer than five messages per month.

Standard Carrier Disclosures

Message and data rates may apply.

Message frequency varies.

For help, contact the league administrator at the address below.

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP or contact the league administrator to have your number removed.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Opt-Out

Members may opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message received from this program, or
  • Contacting the league administrator directly to request removal of their phone number.

Upon opting out, the member's phone number will be removed from the system and no further OTP messages will be sent. Note that opting out will disable login access to the app.

Privacy & Data Use

Phone numbers collected for this program are used solely to deliver OTP authentication messages. They are stored securely in the league's database (Supabase / Postgres) and are not sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party except as required to deliver the SMS message (i.e., the upstream SMS service provider).
