Pindejos Bowling League — Mobile Application

About the App

The Pindejos Bowling League app ("Pindejos") is a private mobile application used by league members to track weekly matchups, scores, standings, RSVPs, season statistics, and the in-app social game economy. This page is the support resource for members who have questions or need help using the app.

Get Help

The fastest way to get support is to email the league administrator. We typically respond within a few days. Please include your league name and a description of the issue so we can help quickly.

Email support: gsblinkhorn@gmail.com

Common Questions

  • I can't sign in / I'm not getting my login code. The app uses one-time passcode (OTP) login delivered by SMS — there is no password. Make sure the phone number you entered matches the one registered with the league, and check that your device has signal. If the code still doesn't arrive, contact the administrator below.
  • How do I join the league? Pindejos is a private app for a single recreational bowling league. Membership and app access are arranged through the league administrator.
  • My scores, standings, or stats look wrong. Pull down to refresh the screen first. If the data still looks incorrect, email the administrator with the week and matchup in question.
  • How do I stop receiving login texts? Reply STOP to any login message, or contact the administrator to have your number removed. See our SMS Consent Policy for details. Note that this disables SMS login access.
  • How do I delete my account or data? You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information at any time by contacting the administrator below.
